About Us

Purpose

During the Movement Control Order (MCO), sanitisation products are becoming a worldwide item of interest to reduce the chances of contracting the Covid-19 disease. In the midst of the pandemic, it has caught our attention that alcohol sanitisers do not account for many factors outside the scope of killing bacterias. Hence, Debac is formed to promote a sanitary lifestyle while offering flexible choices of hand sanitisers to cater to the needs of every individual.

Products

We offer alcohol-free sanitisers which is an alternative to conventional sanitisers. We also offer the good-ol' alcohol-based sanitiser. In addition, both sanitiser types comes in gel and spray form.

Why choose alcohol-free sanitisers?

Alcohol-free sanitisers benefit in aspects of safety and child-friendliness due to its unflammable and unharmful properties. It counters problems faced by skin sensitive people while maintaining the effectiveness of alcohol-based sanitisers. For additional information, please refer ( here )
